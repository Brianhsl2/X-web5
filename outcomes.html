XEnQuad™ outcomes: posture → measurable progress

XEnQuad overlays XEnScore™ (business risk status) with MML View (agent maturity) to determine your posture—and the next move. High XEnScore™ means risk is managed (good); low XEnScore™ means risk is unmanaged (bad). Outcomes improve when maturity rises in the zones that drive exposure.
