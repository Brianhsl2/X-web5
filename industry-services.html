Open protocol foundation: MCP + A2A with evidence‑grade trails

Our protocol approach uses MCP for context‑rich enterprise access and A2A for autonomous coordination, generating evidence‑grade trails that support oversight, internal controls, and regulator expectations.

MCP for context

Standardize access to ERP/CRM/IoT/EHR systems with intent and constraints to reduce brittle integrations.

A2A for coordination

Agents negotiate, delegate, and hand off tasks across domains (security ↔ ops ↔ governance) with shared reasoning context.

Immutable evidence

Signed trails capture why decisions were made, with what context, and by which agents—supporting audit‑ready operation.
