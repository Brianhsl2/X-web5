🟢 Quadrant 4: Aligned Leader

High XEnScore™ (Managed Risk) • High MML (High Capability)

🔴 Quadrant 1: Critical Gap

Low XEnScore™ (Unmanaged Risk) • Low MML (Low Capability)

🔵 Quadrant 2: Vulnerable High‑Tech

Low XEnScore™ (Unmanaged Risk) • High MML (High Capability)

A dangerous paradox: high‑maturity agents, but risks remain unmanaged. Stop building more maturity—realign and redeploy your best agents into the risk zones that actually threaten the business.

XEnQuad overlays XEnScore™ (business risk status) with MML View (agent technical maturity) to place your organization into one of four strategic states—so leaders can decide what to stabilize now, what to automate next, and where to realign investment for the fastest risk reduction and ROI.
